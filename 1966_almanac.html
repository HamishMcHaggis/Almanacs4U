This date also marks
Biodiversity & Harvey Milk Day

Each year, 22 May is the UN's International Day for Biological Diversity, and in California it is Harvey Milk Day, marking the activist born this day in 1930.

Born on May 22
Died on May 22
The thread closes here: May 22, 1966 fell on a Sunday, and its best-known birthday in this almanac is Helena Bonham Carter. Weeks before, Luna 10 reached lunar orbit; weeks after, Surveyor 1 soft-landed on the Moon.

Cost of living
New decimal dollars

Australia changed to decimal currency on 14 February 1966, dollars and cents replacing pounds. Indicative prices: bread about 20c, milk about 19c a litre, eggs about 60c a dozen, with the basic weekly wage near £15 (about $30 new).

Average house price
£3,542

That was the UK average. In the US the median was about $22,900. Reliable Australian capital-city data begins around 1970 (Sydney's median was $18,700 then), so mid-1960s figures are patchy. Indicative.

Who was in charge
Harold Holt

Holt had just become Australia's PM in January. Lyndon B. Johnson held the White House, Harold Wilson Number 10, Queen Elizabeth II the throne, and Pope Paul VI the Church.

Champions of the year
England

England won the World Cup at Wembley. St Kilda claimed their first and only VFL premiership, and Galilee won the Melbourne Cup.

Signs & symbols

The almanac's older trade: the traditional markers tied to the year and to May 22. Folklore rather than science, and offered in that spirit.

Western zodiac
Gemini

The Twins, an air sign ruled by Mercury. May 22 falls in early Gemini, just past the Taurus cusp.

Chinese zodiac
Fire Horse

The lunar year Bing Wu ran from 21 January 1966. Energetic and independent, a sign that comes round once every 60 years.

Birthstone
Emerald

May's stone, a green beryl long tied to rebirth and renewal.

Birth flowers
Lily of the valley

With hawthorn, the traditional blooms of May.

Lucky numbers
3 · 5

Traditionally linked to Gemini; the Horse adds 2, 3 and 7.

Lucky day & colours
Wednesday

Mercury's day. Yellow and green are the Gemini colours.

Numerology
22

The digits of 1966 add up to 22, the very number of the date. It reduces to 4.

That day
Sunday

May 22, 1966 was a Sunday, the 142nd day of the year, in late spring.

Generation
Generation X

Right at the start of Gen X (1965 to 1980), on the cusp of the Baby Boom that closed in 1964.

Life path number
4

Reducing 22 May 1966 (5 + 4 + 4) gives life path 4, the builder: grounded, practical, dependable. The date also carries the master number 22.

Moon that night
Waxing crescent

The new moon had passed only a few nights earlier, so 22 May 1966 rose under a slim waxing crescent.
